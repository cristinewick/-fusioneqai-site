Privacy

Privacy and data posture.

FusionEQ uses the information you provide to respond to consultation, Deal Readiness Report, book, and premium course inquiries.

FusionEQ Deal Readiness Report work is designed around data minimization, anonymized sales context, and only the information needed to understand the decision question.

Details

Customer names and sensitive account details are not needed for an initial FusionEQ Deal Readiness Report conversation.

Contact information
Name, company, role, email, and inquiry details are used to respond to your request.
Sales context
FusionEQ can begin with anonymized or generalized opportunity, presentation, forecast, or stakeholder context. Customer names are not required.
Data scope
Any deeper review is scoped before work begins, with clear agreement on what should and should not be shared.
