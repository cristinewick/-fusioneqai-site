Core Difference

Most systems ask, “Is the process advancing?”
FusionEQ asks, “Is the decision consolidating?”

Traditional sales systems track activity, engagement, methodology progression, and forecast movement. FusionEQ interprets the decision architecture beneath the deal: alignment quality, influence distribution, hidden resistance, momentum integrity, and whether the buying group is actually consolidating toward action.
