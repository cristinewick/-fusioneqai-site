How FusionEQ reads a deal

F

Focus

What actually matters in this deal right now?

U

Understand

What signals suggest something deeper is happening?

S

Structure

Is the deal progressing or just appearing active?

I

Is there real internal influence or assumed support?

O

Is decision ownership clear or still undefined?
