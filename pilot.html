Inspect a Sample Report

Interpretation Pattern

Visible momentum is not always buyer-owned movement.

The report turns that distinction into a practical read: visible signal, identified pattern, and the move that would prove whether the buyer is advancing the decision.

Visible Signal

Meetings continue. The champion is responsive. Next steps exist. The opportunity still appears active.

Identified Pattern

The buyer is cooperating, but not yet carrying the decision. Momentum may be seller-created rather than organization-owned.

Better Move

Test who inside the buyer organization is advancing the decision when your team is not in the room.

This is where the value becomes practical: the team can address the readiness question while the deal can still be influenced.

Report Focus

Choose the business question behind the read.

A report request can focus on one live opportunity, a presentation, a recurring pipeline pattern, or a combined executive question.

Deal Path

Identify what has been evidenced inside an active opportunity and what remains unproven.

Presentation Path

Read whether the message created decision movement, ownership, and a buyer-owned next step.

Both Paths

Use one read when both the opportunity and the message need stronger decision evidence.

Pipeline Pattern

Review repeated patterns across opportunities to identify what the pipeline has not yet proven.
