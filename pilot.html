Interpretation Pattern

Visible momentum is not always decision movement.

The briefing turns that distinction into a simple read: visible signal, undetected pattern, better move.

The result is a sharper test of the decision, not just another next step.

Visible Signal

Meetings continue. The champion is responsive. Next steps exist. The opportunity still appears active.

Undetected Pattern

The buyer is cooperating, but not yet carrying the decision. Momentum may be seller-created rather than organization-owned.

Better Move

Test who inside the buyer organization is advancing the decision when your team is not in the room.

This is where the value becomes practical: fewer surprises between forecast confidence and decision reality.
