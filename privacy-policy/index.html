Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

FusionEQAI respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect, how we may use it, how we may share it, and the choices you may have regarding information you provide.

By using the FusionEQAI website or submitting information to FusionEQAI, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We May Collect

FusionEQAI may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name
  • Company name
  • Role or title
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Information submitted through contact forms, intake forms, report requests, pilot requests, or email communications
  • Anonymized or approved business, opportunity, or deal-readiness context submitted for evaluation or report preparation

FusionEQAI may also collect basic website usage information, such as browser type, device information, pages visited, referring pages, general location data, and analytics information.

2. Information You Should Not Submit

Please do not submit end-user company names, individual stakeholder names, opportunity names, confidential deal identifiers, contract details, sensitive business information, or personal information unless you are authorized to do so.

FusionEQAI intake materials are designed to support anonymized or approved deal-readiness analysis.

You are responsible for ensuring that any information you submit may lawfully and appropriately be shared with FusionEQAI.

3. How We Use Information

FusionEQAI may use information to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Provide requested materials or information
  • Prepare Deal Readiness Reports or related analysis
  • Support pilot, evaluation, customer, or training activities
  • Communicate about FusionEQAI services, materials, or opportunities
  • Improve website content, reports, training materials, and services
  • Maintain business records
  • Protect FusionEQAI rights, materials, website security, and business operations
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Information

FusionEQAI does not sell personal information.

FusionEQAI may share information only when reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide requested services or support business operations
  • Work with trusted service providers who support website, technology, communication, file storage, analytics, or business functions
  • Comply with legal obligations or lawful requests
  • Protect FusionEQAI rights, safety, property, materials, or users
  • Complete a business transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, or transfer of business assets

Service providers are expected to use information only as needed to provide services to FusionEQAI.

5. Anonymized and De-Identified Information

FusionEQAI may use anonymized, aggregated, or de-identified information to improve its frameworks, reports, training materials, services, and website content.

FusionEQAI does not intend to include customer-identifying or confidential end-user information in public materials without permission.

6. Cookies and Analytics

The FusionEQAI website may use cookies, analytics tools, or similar technologies to understand website usage, improve performance, remember preferences, and support website functionality.

You may be able to adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Some website features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

7. Data Security

FusionEQAI takes reasonable steps to protect information submitted through the website or provided for report preparation. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and FusionEQAI cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

FusionEQAI may retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, respond to inquiries, maintain business records, improve materials and services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and protect FusionEQAI rights.

9. Third-Party Websites and Services

The FusionEQAI website may link to third-party websites, tools, or services. FusionEQAI is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, security, or policies of third-party websites or services.

10. Your Choices

You may contact FusionEQAI to request that we update, correct, or delete personal information you have provided, subject to legal, contractual, security, and business recordkeeping requirements.

You may also request to stop receiving non-essential communications from FusionEQAI.

11. Children’s Privacy

FusionEQAI is intended for business and professional audiences. It is not directed to children under 13, and FusionEQAI does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

FusionEQAI may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Your continued use of the website or submission of information after updates are posted means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.

13. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact FusionEQAI through the contact information provided on this website.
