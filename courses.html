How It Fits

The course teaches the Lens. The Diagnostic Pilot applies it to live work.

Education is the learning layer. It helps teams recognize false momentum, weak influence, hidden alignment risk, unclear ownership, and forecast distortion before applying FusionEQ to active opportunities.

Foundations Coverage

The foundations course gives teams the core language for better deal judgment.

Activity vs evidence Separate visible deal motion from evidence of real decision movement.

Metrics vs meaning Understand what reported signals actually mean inside the buying organization.

Decision layer Read the human system beneath pipeline stages, meetings, and updates.

False momentum Notice when activity is continuing but decision readiness is not strengthening.

Influence and ownership Test whether the right people can move the decision internally.

Insight to action Turn a sharper read into better next conversations and leadership questions.
