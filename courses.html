Expanded Foundations

The course also names the decision patterns teams tend to misread.

These patterns connect the course to the newer FusionEQ Deal Lens, Presentation Lens, sample readouts, and pilot paths.

Forecast Distortion

Risk begins before the forecast changes. Teams learn how clean updates can hide weak evidence, unclear ownership, and seller-driven momentum.

Champion Strength

A responsive contact is not always a champion. The course introduces ways to test whether a supporter can create access, alignment, and internal movement.

Hidden Buying Group

The visible participants may not be the full decision system. Teams learn to look for late stakeholders, quiet resistance, and uneven priorities.

Presentation Decision Movement

Engagement is not the same as progress. The course connects deal reading to whether a presentation creates ownership, urgency, alignment, and a meaningful next step.
