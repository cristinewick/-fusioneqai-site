How Education Connects to Live Deal Work

The Deal Readiness Report gives every deal a customized read.

The Deal Readiness Report applies FusionEQ to a specific live opportunity: what is evidenced, what remains unproven, the readiness pattern, and the Recommended Next Move. Education builds the internal capability to make that read more consistently. Foundations introduces the shared language. LENS deepens the language of evidence, narrative, and signals. READ helps sellers interpret those signals before the buyer conversation. CLEAR helps managers turn the read into coaching that improves seller action, review quality, and revenue judgment.

Premium Delivery Formats

The premium courses can be experienced in the format that fits the work.

Executive Briefing A 60- to 90-minute leadership session for recognition, alignment, forecast-quality conversation, and course preview.

Focused Workshop A 2.5- to 4-hour facilitated session for the full model, language practice, workbook application, and selected scenario labs.

Full-Day Application A deeper working session with more discussion, current-deal reflection, manager conversation, and participant synthesis.

Six-Session Cohort A module-by-module experience for distributed teams, reinforcement, field transfer, and practice over time.

Deal-Team Session A focused application using the relevant course module, workbook pages, and live opportunity context.

Deal Review Lab A live or anonymized Deal Readiness Report session that clarifies evidence, unproven assumptions, readiness, and the next buyer-clarity move.

Foundations Coverage

What Foundations introduces

Reading Beyond Pipeline Activity Turn recorded activity into deal readiness intelligence: what is proven, what remains unidentified, and what move would create clarity.

Metrics vs. Meaning Identify what the metric proves, what remains unproven, and what buyer behavior would reveal real decision movement.

The Decision Layer Read whether alignment, influence, ownership, and conviction are strong enough to turn interest into action.

False Momentum Identify whether activity is strengthening decision readiness or simply preserving motion.

Influence and Alignment Test whether the people around the decision can create access, carry the case, and move together.

Insight to Action Translate the read into the next conversation, coaching moment, or customer action that can improve deal movement.
