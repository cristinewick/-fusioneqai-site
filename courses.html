How Education Connects to Live Deal Work

The Deal Readiness Report gives everyone the same read.

Foundations creates the language. READ helps sellers apply it before the buyer conversation. CLEAR helps managers apply it during the review. The Deal Readiness Report brings both conversations back to evidence, readiness, and the Recommended Next Move.

Premium Delivery Formats

READ and CLEAR can be delivered through facilitated formats that match the team’s goals.

Executive Workshop A concentrated facilitated session for leaders, sellers, managers, or deal teams.

Guided Online Cohort A multi-session group experience with practice, discussion, field application, and reinforcement.

In-House Team Facilitation A deeper engagement connected to one company’s deal context, review cadence, and active pipeline conversations.

Deal Review Facilitation A live facilitated review using the Deal Readiness Report to clarify evidence, readiness, and the Recommended Next Move.

Foundations Coverage

What Foundations introduces

Reading Beyond Pipeline Activity Turn recorded activity into deal readiness intelligence: what is proven, what remains unidentified, and what move would create clarity.

Metrics vs. Meaning Identify what the metric proves, what remains unproven, and what buyer behavior would reveal real decision movement.

The Decision Layer Read whether alignment, influence, ownership, and conviction are strong enough to turn interest into action.

False Momentum Identify whether activity is strengthening decision readiness or simply preserving motion.

Influence and Alignment Test whether the people around the decision can create access, carry the case, and move together.

Insight to Action Translate the read into the next conversation, coaching moment, or customer action that can improve deal movement.
