A customer-facing sample showing how FusionEQ moves from visible activity to decision evidence, buyer ownership, readiness pattern, and Recommended Next Move.

Sample Format

This anonymized sample uses a Deal Memory ID rather than a customer name. The structure is designed for executive review: clear score, evidenced reality, unproven assumptions, decision-readiness pattern, and next move.

What to notice: the report begins with the readiness score, identified pattern, and Recommended Next Move, then expands into the evidence, interpretation, and questions that create decision clarity. It does not summarize activity. It explains what the activity means.
