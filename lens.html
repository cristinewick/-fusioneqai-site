What Changes

The conversation moves from activity to readiness.

01 Traditional review asks what happened.

Is there a next step? Did the meeting happen? Is the stakeholder responsive? Is the forecast still stable?

02 FusionEQ asks what is not yet confirmed.

Where is readiness incomplete? Who is carrying the decision? What pattern needs attention before the forecast changes?

03 The next move becomes clearer.

The team can respond to the readiness constraint while the deal can still be influenced.
