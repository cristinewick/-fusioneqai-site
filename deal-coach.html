Execution Leverage

What coaching focuses on.

01

Focus the team

Identify the one constraint most likely to stall, dilute, or kill momentum.

02

Script the conversation

Shape the language for quote pressure, budget truth, hidden blockers, and decision ownership.

03

Regain control

Move from reacting to buyer requests toward driving alignment, timing, and decision sequence.
