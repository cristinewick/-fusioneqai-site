The Shift

From the pipeline story to the decision story.

The pipeline can tell you what has been recorded. The harder question is what those signals mean inside the buying organization.

Author

Cristine Wick

Cristine Wick is the Founder and Creator of FusionEQ AI and the author of The Deal Behind the Deal. She brings more than 30 years of enterprise sales experience to a question most revenue teams recognize: what is really happening inside a deal before the outcome becomes visible?

Before entering sales, she studied Broadcast News Journalism with a minor in Psychology. That background shaped the lens of the book: ask better questions, work with incomplete information, and read the human dynamics behind the visible story.

Her work now sits at the intersection of emotional intelligence, artificial intelligence, and enterprise revenue judgment: helping sellers and leaders see what most teams miss before the forecast makes it obvious.
