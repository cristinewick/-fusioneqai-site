Cristine Wick

Cristine Wick brings more than 30 years of enterprise sales experience to a question most revenue teams recognize: what is really happening inside a deal before the outcome becomes visible?

Before entering sales, she studied Broadcast News Journalism with a minor in Psychology and interned at KNSD Channel 39. That training shaped her instinct for asking better questions, working with incomplete information, and reading the human dynamics beneath the visible story.

Her work now sits at the intersection of emotional intelligence, artificial intelligence, and enterprise revenue judgment: helping sellers and leaders read alignment, influence, urgency, ownership, and decision readiness more clearly.
