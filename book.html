Core Idea

The pipeline tells you what is happening. It does not tell you what it means.

Deals don’t usually fail all at once. They stall, slip, or drift before the forecast catches up.

Visible Deal
  • Stage is right
  • Activity is there
  • Next steps are defined
Deal Behind the Deal
  • Influence is assumed
  • Alignment is incomplete
  • Decision ownership is unclear

Strategic Read

The problem isn’t your sales system.
It’s what your systems can’t interpret.
