CLEAR Framework

A practical review model for turning report insight into seller success.

Clarify

Clarify what has been recorded. Honor the salesperson's work and organize the visible context.

Locate

Locate what has been evidenced. Ground confidence in buyer behavior, not only seller effort or internal belief.

Examine

Examine what remains unproven. Identify the assumption that would most improve the deal read if validated.

Advance

Advance the buyer-clarity move. Turn insight into a stronger next buyer conversation.

Recalibrate

Recalibrate the revenue read. Communicate confidence with precision based on evidence and remaining uncertainty.
