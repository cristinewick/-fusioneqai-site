Signature Model

Recorded. Evidence. Assumption. Decision.

Recorded

What has been recorded? What is visible? What has been captured in meetings, notes, activity, and buyer interaction?

Evidence

What has been evidenced by buyer behavior? What proves movement, ownership, alignment, urgency, influence, or readiness?

Assumption

What remains unproven? Which assumption still needs validation before confidence is fully supportable?

Decision

What next move would create decision clarity? What action, question, or buyer-owned step would create the next piece of evidence?
